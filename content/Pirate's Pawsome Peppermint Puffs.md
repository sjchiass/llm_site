These bite-sized treats are made with crushed peppermint candies, powdered sugar, and a hint o' vanilla extract. They're so easy to make and fun to eat that ye'll be hooked from the first bite!

**Recipe:**

Ingredients:

* 1 cup crushed peppermint candies
* 1/2 cup powdered sugar
* 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
* 1/4 teaspoon salt
* 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened

Instructions:

1. Preheat yer ship's galley to 350°F (180°C).
2. In a medium bowl, mix together crushed peppermint candies and powdered sugar.
3. Add vanilla extract and salt to the bowl; stir until combined.
4. Melt the butter in a separate saucepan over low heat.
5. Pour the melted butter into the peppermint mixture; stir until a dough forms.
6. Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface to about 1/4 inch thickness.
7. Use a cookie cutter or a glass to cut out shapes from the dough.
8. Place the puffs on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until lightly golden.

**Tips from PugBeard:**

* For an extra-festive touch, roll the cooled puffs in crushed candy canes before servin'.
* Store leftover puffs in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days.
