**A Final Recipe:**

As a parting gift, I'll share one final recipe with ye. It's a pirate pug classic:

**Pirate Pupcorn**

Ingredients:

* 1 cup popcorn kernels
* 2 tablespoons coconut oil
* 1 teaspoon sea salt

Instructions:

1. Pop the popcorn and set it aside.
2. In a separate pan, heat the coconut oil over medium heat.
3. Add the sea salt to the hot oil and stir until combined.
4. Pour the seasoned oil mixture over the popped popcorn and toss until coated.
